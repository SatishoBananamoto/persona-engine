## Intensity Threshold Assessment

Our thresholds: 0.15 / 0.25 / 0.35 / 0.45 | 0.55 / 0.65 / 0.75 / 0.85

BFI-2 norms (Soto & John 2017, US adult sample):
- Mean scores on 1-5 scale center around 3.0-3.5 depending on trait
- Standard deviation ≈ 0.7-0.8

Mapping to our 0-1 scale (linear transform from 1-5):
- 1 SD below mean ≈ 0.35 on our scale
- 1 SD above mean ≈ 0.65 on our scale
- 2 SD ≈ 0.20 / 0.80

Our thresholds roughly correspond to:
- 0.55/0.45: within 0.5 SD of mean (mild)
- 0.65/0.35: ~1 SD from mean (moderate)
- 0.75/0.25: ~1.5 SD from mean (strong)
- 0.85/0.15: ~2 SD from mean (very strong)

This is psychometrically reasonable — 1 SD is "noticeable", 2 SD is "very prominent."
